Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start using VoiceTranslate Pro?

Getting started is easy:

  • Visit our homepage and scroll to the translation section
  • Select your source and target languages from the dropdown menus
  • Click "Start Speaking" and allow microphone access when prompted
  • Speak clearly into your microphone
  • Click "Stop Recording" when finished
  • Your translation will appear automatically with audio playback

No registration or download is required!

What languages are supported?

VoiceTranslate Pro supports over 100 languages including:

  • European: English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Swedish, Norwegian, Danish, Finnish, Polish, Czech, Greek, Romanian
  • Asian: Chinese (Mandarin & Cantonese), Japanese, Korean, Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Thai, Vietnamese, Indonesian, Malay
  • Middle Eastern: Arabic, Hebrew, Persian, Turkish, Urdu
  • Other: Russian, Ukrainian, Swahili, Zulu, Afrikaans, and many more

We continuously add new languages based on user demand.

Why isn't my microphone working?

If your microphone isn't working, try these solutions:

  • Check that you've granted microphone permission to your browser
  • Ensure your microphone is properly connected and not muted
  • Try using a supported browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari, Firefox)
  • Close other applications that might be using your microphone
  • Restart your browser and try again
  • Check your system audio settings to ensure the correct microphone is selected

Still having issues? Contact our support team for personalized assistance.

How accurate are the translations?

Our AI-powered translation system achieves 95%+ accuracy for most language pairs. Accuracy depends on several factors:

  • Audio quality and clarity of speech
  • Background noise levels
  • Complexity of the content being translated
  • Language pair (some combinations are more accurate than others)
  • Use of idioms, slang, or technical terminology

We continuously improve our AI models to deliver better accuracy across all languages.

Is my data safe and private?

Absolutely! Your privacy is our top priority:

  • We do NOT store your voice recordings on our servers
  • All audio processing happens in real-time with immediate deletion
  • Translation history is stored locally on your device only
  • We use end-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • We never share your data with third parties
  • We are fully compliant with GDPR and CCPA regulations

Read our Privacy Policy for complete details.

Can I use VoiceTranslate Pro offline?

Currently, VoiceTranslate Pro requires an internet connection to function because:

  • Translation processing uses cloud-based AI models
  • Speech recognition requires server-side processing
  • Voice synthesis needs real-time data access

We're working on an offline mode for limited functionality in future updates. Stay tuned!

Yes! VoiceTranslate Pro is 100% free with no hidden costs:

  • No registration or account required
  • Unlimited translations
  • Access to all 100+ languages
  • All features included
  • No ads or premium tiers
  • No credit card required

We believe language should never be a barrier to communication.

What browsers are supported?

VoiceTranslate Pro works best on modern browsers that support the Web Speech API:

  • Google Chrome (Recommended) - Version 80+
  • Microsoft Edge - Version 80+
  • Safari - Version 14+ (macOS and iOS)
  • Firefox - Version 80+ (limited speech recognition)
  • Opera - Version 67+

For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of Chrome or Edge.

How can I improve translation accuracy?

Follow these tips for the best results:

  • Speak clearly and at a moderate pace
  • Use a quality microphone in a quiet environment
  • Minimize background noise and echoes
  • Speak in complete sentences rather than fragments
  • Avoid heavy accents or unclear pronunciation when possible
  • Position your microphone 6-12 inches from your mouth
  • Use headphones to prevent audio feedback

Better audio input leads to better translation output!

Can I save my translation history?

Yes! Translation history is automatically saved locally on your device:

  • View your last 10 translations in the history section
  • Copy any previous translation with one click
  • Clear history anytime for privacy
  • History is stored using browser localStorage
  • Data stays on your device - never uploaded to servers

Note: Clearing browser data or cookies will delete your history.
